# **App Name**: TechnoWave Agency

## Core Features:

- Services Overview: Comprehensive listing of web development services offered, detailing each service's capabilities and benefits.
- Portfolio Showcase: Interactive display of past projects, each with descriptions, visuals, and client testimonials. A generative AI powered "inspiration tool" that generates website mockup for your next project. This AI tool lets you compare and get inspiration from previously successful projects in your niche.
- About Us: Detailed introduction to the agency's team, values, and development philosophy.
- Contact Form: User-friendly form for potential clients to inquire about services or request consultations.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Forest green (#84C87B), representing growth, innovation, and a nod to technological progress. A lighter shade will serve as the secondary.
- Background color: Dark navy blue (#083751), evokes trust and expertise in a technology-driven industry. Slightly desaturated from the primary.
- Accent color: Teal (#43A873), used strategically for interactive elements to draw user attention and enhance engagement.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk', a modern sans-serif, conveys a tech-forward feel and ensure legibility for key headings.
- Body font: 'Inter', a clean, readable sans-serif, ensuring readability and a professional aesthetic.
- Modern line icons, reflecting services and technology-related themes. Animated on hover.
- Grid-based layout, creating a structured, clean and modern look that reflects the organized nature of the company.
- Subtle, interactive animations, enhancing user experience and modern feel. Focus on smooth transitions and loading animations.
